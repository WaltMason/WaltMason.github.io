The Parametric Conundrum.

  • August 5, 2018
  • In Knowledge Revit

Parameters can be a bit confusing in Revit, sometimes even to the seasoned user. For the new to Revit user they can seem like another language, especially in regard to parameter types, schedules, and how they work. It doesn’t help that in a sense, there are three types of types of parameters, confusion intended.

To clear things up decided I needed to put a guide together. The problem is that as soon as I started to put it together I realized that parameters in general have so many facets, that to explain them all would need to be more of a book than a guide, and frankly even I would be bored reading a book on parameters. Therefore, I decided to break it down into a series of mini guides based on both of your level of experience and then add some separate resources that break them down. That way you can get quick overviews or go directly into the areas you need.
